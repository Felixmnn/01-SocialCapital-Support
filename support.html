Important Note on Data Responsibility

Social Capital runs completely standalone on your device. There is no server connection and no way for us to access your app data. Please create your own backups and manage or delete your data directly within the app.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I back up my data?

Backups must be created independently by the user, as no cloud synchronization is provided by Social Capital.

How do I delete my data?

Instructions for complete local deletion can be found on the Account & Data Deletion page.

Why may results differ from reality?

The app calculates relationships from limited daily entries. As a result, results may contain errors and do not fully reflect reality.

Safety and Responsibility Guidelines

Social Capital is a local standalone app with no server connection. These notes support a safe and responsible handling of your data and results.

Responsible Use

  • App results serve as guidance, not as objective truth.
  • Please do not make sensitive decisions based solely on app values.
  • Ensure accurate and fair entries to reduce distortions.

Data Security

Since no Social Capital servers are used, your app data remains locally on your device. Protecting and backing up the data is your responsibility.

Quality of Results

  • Daily input time is limited to 2 minutes.
  • Not all real-world influencing factors can be captured.
  • Deviations from the actual situation are likely.
