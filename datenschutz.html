Privacy Policy

As of: May 1, 2026

1. Contact

Email: support.socialcapital@gmail.com

2. Data We Process

Social Capital is a fully standalone app with no server connection. Usage takes place locally on the end device.

  • Entries made by the user in the app (stored locally).
  • Locally calculated factors, e.g. trust, attention, support.
  • Local app settings.

These data are not transferred to Social Capital servers.

3. Purposes and Legal Bases

  • Local processing to provide app functionality (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR, where applicable).

4. Recipients and Service Providers

No app user data is transmitted to Social Capital or to external service providers, as no server-side processing takes place.

5. Third-Country Transfers

No third-country transfers are carried out by Social Capital.

6. Retention Period

Data is stored exclusively locally on the end device and remains there until the user deletes it.

  • No server-side retention periods at Social Capital.
  • Backups are entirely the responsibility of the user.

7. Rights of Data Subjects

Under the GDPR, you have in particular the following rights:

  • Access (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Objection (Art. 21 GDPR)
  • Withdrawal of granted consents
  • Complaint to a data protection supervisory authority

As Social Capital has no access to locally stored app data, data-related actions can only be carried out directly on the end device by the user themselves.

8. Account & Data Deletion

Information on deleting your account and personal data can be found on the Account & Data Deletion page.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect legal, technical, or functional changes. The version published here at any time shall be authoritative.
