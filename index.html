Social Capital: Trust, Attention, Support

An app to measure and improve the quality of your social relationships. All data stays on your device – no third parties, no cloud, no transfers.

Google Play: Privacy Policy Apple: App Privacy Details Support URL Account Deletion Offline / No Server Access

How the App Works

Users have 2 minutes per day to log activities related to other people. Based on these entries, the app calculates factors such as trust, attention, support, and relationship development.

The results are intended as guidance. The analysis may contain errors and does not represent an objective or complete picture of reality, as not all relevant factors can be captured.
